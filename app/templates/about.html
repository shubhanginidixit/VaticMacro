About VaticMacro

Indian macroeconomic inflation research, in one dashboard.

VaticMacro combines historical macroeconomic data, feature engineering, and forecasting models to let you inspect inflation trends, compare indicators, and test simple scenarios from the browser.

Dashboard

Live KPI cards, inflation history, and market context.

Analysis

Correlation matrices and indicator comparisons.

Predict

Adjust WPI, rates, and FX to test inflation scenarios.

What to run

Start the app with py run.py or python app.py, then open http://127.0.0.1:5000. The dashboard, analysis, models, predict, forecast, and about pages are linked from the sidebar.
